About Me

My name is Nada Elshafey. I was born and raised in Cairo, Egypt and I am currently doing my Bachelor's degree in the US. I am doing a double major in Growth and Structure of Cities, and Fine Arts at Bryn Mawr College in Bryn Mawr, PA. Outside class, I love to design and build. I have worked with so many mediums from pencils and charcoals to wood and metal. You can view many of my creations in my portfolio. I enjoy travelling and seeing new places and I am looking forward to visit more cities to write about in my blog. Have fun browsing my website!
